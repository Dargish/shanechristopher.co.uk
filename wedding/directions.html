From London:



Take the M4 to Bristol, from there head south on the M5 until past Exeter where you go onto the A30.
Follow signs for Cheriton Bishop, take a left in the middle of the village opposite a circular seating area:


Follow this small lane around a bend to the right, at the next bend there will be a turnoff to the right.


Take this turn but be careful to watch for potholes in the road, the farm is at the end of the lane

Good luck!
